# uPorta: Automated, Multipurpose and Sustainable Dispensing System

uPorta is an automated dispensing system that provides immediate access to a wide range of products—from fresh food and hot meals to clothing, books, cleaning or hardware items—via 24/7 autonomous points of sale. The approach blends **industrial design engineering**, **human–machine interaction**, and **sustainability** to reduce shopping frictions and support local economies.

## Context

In modern cities, well-designed automation integrates into daily life, offering frictionless access to essential goods and extending store hours continuously.

## Key features

- **Multiproduct and modular:** differentiated conditions per module (refrigerated, heated, ambient, frozen), adjustable trays/elevators, and interchangeable components to reconfigure quickly.
- **Advanced automation:** IoT real-time inventory, contactless payments, multilingual touchscreen with information and recommendations.
- **User-centered design:** ergonomics and accessibility (wheelchair-reachable heights, audio guidance, high contrast UI, Braille).
- **Safety and hygiene:** robust materials, automated sanitization (e.g., UV/ventilation), and age/ID checks for regulated items.
- **Sustainability:** efficient insulation, low-power LEDs, standby modes, modular design for longevity and easy repair.

## Benefits

- **24/7 availability** and fast, line-free purchases.
- **Universal accessibility** with multiple interaction modes (touch, mobile, voice).
- **All-in-one convenience** (food, apparel, hardware, books…).
- **Transparent information**: local origin, ingredients, specs, sizes, and reviews.

## Design, sustainability, and local impact

The development follows **sustainable industrial design** principles and a neutral, modern aesthetic to fit the urban fabric. As a platform open to local producers and small businesses, it shortens supply chains, lowers carbon footprint, and strengthens proximity consumption.
